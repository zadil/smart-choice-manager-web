Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Smart Choice Manager ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Google Workspace add-on.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Form Data

Our add-on only accesses the following data within your Google Workspace environment:

  • Structure and content of your Google Forms
  • Responses submitted to your forms
  • Configuration settings you define in the add-on
  • Tracking data stored in associated Google Sheets

2.2 Technical Data

We do not collect any technical or analytics data. The add-on operates entirely within your Google Workspace environment.

3. Data Usage

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Managing response limits in your forms
  • Tracking response counts for each option
  • Automatically updating choice availability
  • Providing configuration and management features

4. Storage and Security

4.1 Data Location

All your data remains within your Google Workspace environment. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

4.2 Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Google Workspace's secure infrastructure
  • Standard Google Apps Script security protocols
  • Limited access to only data necessary for operation
  • No external data transmission

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties. Your data remains under your complete control within your Google Workspace environment.

6. Required Permissions

The add-on requests the following permissions:

  • Google Forms: To read and modify your form structure
  • Google Sheets: To create and manage response tracking sheets
  • Google Drive: To access associated form and sheet files
  • User Interface: To display configuration menus and dialogs

7. Your Rights

You retain complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible in your Google Forms and Sheets
  • Modification: You can modify or delete your data at any time
  • Deletion: You can uninstall the add-on and delete all associated data
  • Portability: Your data remains in standard Google formats

8. Compliance

Our add-on complies with:

  • Google Workspace Terms of Service
  • Google Apps Script security policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Local data protection regulations

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised update date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or data processing, you can contact us:

Email: adil.zouhal@gmail.com

Documentation: GitHub Repository

11. Uninstallation

To uninstall the add-on and remove all associated data:

  1. Open Google Forms
  2. Access Add-ons in the menu
  3. Select "Manage add-ons"
  4. Find Smart Choice Manager and click "Remove"
  5. Manually delete created tracking sheets if desired
